Mexican cusine


Sal y Limon Mexican Cuisine is a fully licensed Mexican restaurant in East Vancouver, offering take out service, as well as a fun atmosphere for dining in with friends and family. We don’t take reservations, so drop by anytime to enjoy a cool beer with your tacos, check out our unique artwork and let the kids loose in our play area.



Chinese cusine


Kirin Restaurant, showcasing the finest Chinese Cuisine in Vancouver and served in a warm, authentic and elegant atmosphere. Indulge in our award-winning dim sum prepared fresh daily. Experience our exquisite seafood dishes; Kirin's culinary range is unsurpassed. Add this to our renowned attentive, personal service and it's easy to see why Kirin Restaurant is praised by critics as one of the best premium Chinese dining experiences in North America.


Korean cusine


SURA has been delivering excellent Korean dining experience to connoisseurs of great food in Vancouver since 2008. While appreciating the interior that weaves a modern aesthetic through Korean traditional atmosphere, you can expect the fineness of sophisticated authentic yet contemporary Korean cuisine by our master chef Sang-jo Han.

SURA is moving into its sixth year with a flourish: the restaurant has received its third Vancouver Restaurant Awards from Vancouver Magazine and reviews continue to extol its delights. Our commitment to authentic Korean Royal Cuisine makes SURA the ideal dining destination for Vancouverites and visitors alike and our attentive staff will ensure that your event is memorable. You will enjoy what the best korean restaurant in Vancouver has to offer.


Thai cusine


Salathai keeps with the tradition of Thai culture and customs, they celebrate the concept of sanuk (the idea that life should be fun). You will find delicious food, good cheer, beer towers and laughter in full force. We don't just work together, we celebrate together.

Specializing in Central Thailand cuisine, combning flavours customary to Thai food: spicy, sour, sweet, salty and bitter, with an exceedingly royal flair. Having served the Thai Royal Family, the dishes are all prepared and presented in much the same manner as befitting royalty.
